About Swati

A seeker, a student, a teacher.

Swati came to yoga not through a studio but through a lineage — a tradition passed through culture, language, and story. Her practice is inseparable from the philosophy and heritage from which it grew.

She completed her 200-hour certification at Soma Yoga with Amy Leydon and Todd Skoglund. Her personal practice is rooted in the teachings of Vipassana meditation and Sadhguru's Inner Engineering program.

Swati draws inspiration from her Indian cultural upbringing — the stories and teachings of ancient texts and modern interpretations, the wisdom of Ayurveda, and the beauty of South Asian artists, poets, and musicians.

She also brings a rigorous academic foundation to her teaching: degrees from Yale and Stanford, and a role as Associate Professor of Musculoskeletal Radiology — bridging ancient wisdom with modern scientific understanding.
